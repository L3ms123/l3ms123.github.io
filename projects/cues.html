Uncertainty & Negation Detection of cues in Medical Texts

  • Tech Stack: Python, NLP, spaCy, Scikit-learn, Pickle

This project focuses on detecting uncertainty and negation cues in medical texts written in Catalan and Spanish. The goal is to identify sentences where medical statements are uncertain or negated to improve information extraction for clinical applications.

The system uses Natural Language Processing techniques including tokenization, linguistic feature extraction, and a CRF machine learning classifier. It has been tested on annotated clinical corpora in both languages and shows promising results for clinical text mining.
